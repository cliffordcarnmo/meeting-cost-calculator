Meeting Cost Calculator

A tool to help you calculate the cost of a meeting, and make you think of it's value.

TIPS FOR BETTER MEETINGS:
  • Do we really need to have a meeting? Could an email/teams message or a video recording be better instead?
  • Have an agenda for the meeting - this allows the contestants to judge if they need to attend the meeting or not
  • Think about what the meeting is for? What is the purpose? What do you to achieve? How do we know when we are done?
    • Think of meetings as investments and consider the cost when making such an investment, this tool allow you to make a better decision
